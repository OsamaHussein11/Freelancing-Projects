Young Artists Share Stories Online

By Osama Hussein | August 3, 2025

A group of young artists in Gaza are using social media to share drawings, short videos, and personal stories. Their work reflects daily life, hope, and resilience.

Even with limited tools, they continue to express themselves and connect with audiences outside Gaza. Art has become a way to document moments and emotions.

Many followers support these artists by sharing their work and encouraging them to continue creating content.
