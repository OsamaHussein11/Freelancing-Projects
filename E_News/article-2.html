Power Outages Hit Gaza Again

By Osama Hussein | August 2, 2025

Electricity cuts continue to affect hospitals, schools, homes, and small businesses in Gaza. Many families depend on batteries, solar panels, or generators when available.

The unstable power supply makes daily life more difficult, especially for people who need medical devices or internet access for study and work.

Residents are trying to manage their daily schedules around limited electricity hours, while small businesses are searching for safer and cheaper backup solutions.
