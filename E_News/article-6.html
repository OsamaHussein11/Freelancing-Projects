Students Use Online Learning to Keep Studying

By Osama Hussein | August 4, 2025

Many university students are trying to continue their education through online resources, recorded lectures, and small study groups.

Internet problems and electricity cuts make learning difficult, but students still use available tools to practice programming, improve English, and complete assignments.

Simple digital platforms help them stay connected with teachers and classmates, and many students use free resources to improve their technical skills.
