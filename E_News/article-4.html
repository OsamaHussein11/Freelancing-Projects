Youth Football League Resumes in Gaza

By Osama Hussein | August 2, 2025

Local youth football activities have returned in some areas, giving young players a chance to practice and enjoy sports again.

Coaches say that sports can help children and teenagers reduce stress and build teamwork. The matches are simple, but they bring positive energy to the community.

Parents and volunteers are helping organize safe spaces for training, even with limited equipment and difficult conditions.
